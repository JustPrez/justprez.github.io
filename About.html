About us

Why Choose Us?

H2Go Rockets was born out of a need for young adults to get hands-on experience of their own in engineering and physics, the water rockets we produce are intended to help those in higher education to grasp a fundamental understanding of what being an engineer is all about! Our products are designed from the ground up with precision and care. We are committed to delivering high-quality kits that not only educate but also entertain. Making use of simple parts and a bottle you can find almost anywhere, our kits are designed to be assembled with ease.

Our Mission

At H2Go Rockets, our mission is to inspire the next generation of engineers and scientists by providing innovative and educational water rocket kits. We believe in hands-on learning and fostering creativity through practical experimentation and exploration. Our kits are designed to be to be both fun and engaging, encouraging students to think critically and problem-solve in a collaborative environment. We are committed to providing high-quality products that are simple to use and easy to understand, making learning fun for everyone.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, H2Go Rockets started as a small project aimed at making engineering concepts accessible and fun for students. We want to inspire the next generation of engineers and scientists by providing hands-on learning experiences that are both educational and entertaining. Our water rocket kits were designed with you in mind, whether you're a teacher or a student, our products were made for anyone to explore.
