Welcome to H2Go Rockets

Teaching young adults enginnering!

At H2Go Rockets, we inspire young minds to explore the wonders of engineering and science through hands-on learning and exciting projects. Our mission is to ignite curiosity and creativity in the next generation of innovators.

Join us as we embark on a journey to discover the principles of rocket science, build amazing projects, and have fun along the way!

What We Offer

Rocket Building Kits

Learn the basics of rocket design and construction with our easy to use water rocket kit.

Science Experiments

Explore the principles of physics and chemistry with an exciting experiment and delve deep into the world of kinematics. The kit lets you get a fundemental grasp on the forces acting on the rocket!

Learn Coding!

In order to track the movement of the rocket, the kit includes a microbit and a guide on how to program it to track the rockets movement.

Get Involved

Whether you're a student, parent, or educator, there's a place for you at H2Go Rockets. Join our community and start your journey into the world of engineering today!
